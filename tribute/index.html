A TRIBUTE TO OUR NATIONAL HEROES

INDIAN ARMY

Saluting the valiant souls who stand strong,
Defending the nation, selfless and long,
With unwavering courage, they face every test,
In their hands, we find security and rest.

Through heat and cold, they march with pride,
On the borders, where their sacrifices abide,
Guarding the frontiers with hearts made of steel,
For our safety and freedom, they zealously feel.

In times of peace, and when storms cloud the sky,
They're the ones who answer each desperate cry,
From mountains to deserts, they stand as one,
With unity and purpose, their battle is won.

Remember the tales of heroes now gone,
Their legacy lives as the dawn breaks on,
From Kargil's peaks to the depths of the sea,
They've shown what it means to be truly free.

To the Indian Army, we raise our voice,
In honor and gratitude, we make our choice,
For the sacrifices made, the battles they fight,
In the day and the dark, their beacon of light.

So let's stand together, hand in hand,
And remember those who protect our land,
With respect and admiration, our hearts shall soar,
To the Indian soldiers, our forevermore.

Salute to the Indian Army!
